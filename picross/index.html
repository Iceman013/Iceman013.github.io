Instructions

Fill in the entire board based on the numbers on the sides.

Each number represents consecutive black cells in that row or column.

The consecutive black cells are in order based on the order of the numbers in the header.

Listen, I made picross. You asked for this. You should know how this works.

It might seem like there are 2 possible solutions for a board sometimes. If you do make a board that matches all the numbers, that will count as a success.

Controls

Left click to turn a cell on.

Right click to turn a cell off.

Click again to reset a cell to blank.

Click and drag to select rows or columns quickly.
