UGA Juggling Club

Welcome to the Juggling Club at University of Georgia! Founded in the fall of 2019, we love to promote the art and sport of juggling by meeting weekly to share tricks, socialize, teach newcomers, and most importantly- juggle!

We're as social as we are talented, and welcome all skill levels. Don't have juggling props? No problem! We've got dozens of extra props on hand that members can juggle with. Our membership ranges from individuals with no juggling experience to routine performers, and more skilled members love giving free juggling lessons to beginners. All juggling disciplines are welcome here, from toss juggling, diablo, poi, devil sticks, fire staff, contact juggling, yo-yo, and any other circus genre that calls us home. We're excited to meet you, and juggle away!

Meetings are held every Monday at 4:30pm on North Campus behind UGA's Old College. Feel free to come and go as you please, and we'll nearly always be done by 6:00pm.
