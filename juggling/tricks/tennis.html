Prerequisites

These are recommended tricks to learn before you do this one. It may help.

    Tutorial

    To begin, do an inside cascade pattern. On every third throw, throw outside. The ball that goes outside each time is the same so it should feel different.

    Tips

    • A different colored ball can help you see the one that goes on the outside.
    • As you get better and are able to do this more cleanly, making a more exagerated motion can help it look better.
