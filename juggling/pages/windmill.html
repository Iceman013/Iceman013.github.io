Windmills

Description

This trick results in the balls going in a circle pattern. This makes a windmill design.

Prerequisites

These are recommended tricks to learn before you do this one. It may help.
  • Under the Arm

Tutorial

This trick should be started from scratch. It begins with a dominant throw. The arms then cross so non-dominant goes below dominant. Non-dominant throws across. This is the opposite direction it usually throws. The non-dominant goes back and catches the ball in the air. This was the first one thrown. This is where things begin to loop. Throw from dominant hand. This time, when you cross arms to throw from non-dominant, catch the ball in the air with you dominant hand. This is a right, left, right, left ... pattern. The rhythm is the same as a cascade except moving your arms more.

Tips

  • Ensure you throw across and not just up. This will give the trick more effect.
