Prerequisites

These are recommended tricks to learn before you do this one. It may help.

    Tutorial

    This pattern is similar to classic 4 in the sense that balls do not swap hands. It can also be described as a 3-Ball Mill's Mess with an extra ball inserted in the pattern. Although this seems obvious, this thinking can be helpful to understand the trick.

    Tips

    • This trick requires a lot of practice to get down smooth.
