Rings

Description

Prerequisites

These are recommended tricks to learn before you do this one. It may help.

    Tutorial

    To start, hold two in one hand and one in the other. Rings should be pinched when holding them. As you throw them, add a little backspin to give them stability. Catches are done similarly with a pinch catch.

    Tips

    • Most ball tricks can be done with rings although some look better than others.
    • This motion of pinching and spinning allows more random objects to be juggled.
    • Be careful how you hold you hand during catches. Rings can hit your thumbs and hurt.
