Boston Mess

Description

This trick has all 3 balls just go up and down while the hands go back and forth to support them.

Prerequisites

These are recommended tricks to learn before you do this one. It may help.
  • 3-Ball Mill's Mess
  • Columns

Tutorial

This trick is a right, left, right, left etc pattern. The only change is hand placement and throws. Every throw goes straight up. The hands go across positions catching and rethrowing the ball.

Tips

  • Staying focused on the middle ball can help prevent accidentally swapping the positions.
  • As you practice more, you can made the pattern wider so the effect is more obvious.
