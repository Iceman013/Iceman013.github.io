Three in One Hand

Description

This is one of the harder 3-ball tricks because it only uses one hand. It becomes part of other high ball tricks.

Prerequisites

These are recommended tricks to learn before you do this one. It may help.

    Tutorial

    This trick is really simple in that it only requires one motion from one hand. To start, hold all three in one hand and give a good high throw. Continue throwing with good height consistently. This will have a rotational pattern instead of just columns.

    Tips

    • This trick will require a lot of height. In order to effectively maintain the pattern, you will need consistency and height. Although conceptually easy, this trick takes some time to learn due to this.
