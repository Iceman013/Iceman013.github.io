Prerequisites

These are recommended tricks to learn before you do this one. It may help.

    Tutorial

    Step 1

    To begin, practice the 3-ball snake. This is a 3-ball cascade with 2 gaps in it. This will help develop the rhythm and pacing.

    Step 2

    Next, practice the 4-ball snake. This replaces one of the gaps from the previous step.

    Step 3

    To start the pattern, you will need three in one hand and two in the other. From there, it is a right, left, right, left etc pattern.

    Tips

    • Height and consistency are key.
    • This takes speed and strength. It can be easier to use somewhat lighter balls that are softer in order to prevent arm pain.

    Fun Facts

    • Before people learned this pattern, the typical 5-ball pattern was two in one hand and three in the other.
