Under the Arm

Description

This simple trick is just throwing the ball under your arm. This is a one motion trick that can be thrown in to a three ball pattern to spice it up.

Prerequisites

These are recommended tricks to learn before you do this one. It may help.
  • Cascade

Tutorial

To do this trick, start with the cascade (or reverse cascade, both work). Then, on the non-dominant throw, throw up instead of across. Move your dominant hand under the other arm. Then throw that ball and catch the one in the air. Return both hands to their normal positions.

Tips

  • This trick does not require a special start. It can be thrown in while doing cascade. Because of this, you can take your time to line up a good start.
