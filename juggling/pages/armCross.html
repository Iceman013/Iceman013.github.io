Arms Crossed

Description

This trick is crossing your arms and juggling cascade.

Prerequisites

These are recommended tricks to learn before you do this one. It may help.
  • Under the Arm

Tutorial

Cross your arms. This is usually done with the dominant arm on the bottom. Then proceed to do the classic cascade without breaking out of the crossed position. Your reflexes will make you want to uncross your arms.

Tips

  • This trick feels weird and unatural when you do it. It will take a great deal of practice before it feels normal and looks clean.
